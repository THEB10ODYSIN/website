Welcome

Austin & Bell is a funeral home and cremation service based in Springfield, Tennessee, established in 1842, making it the oldest funeral home in the state. It serves several locations in Tennessee and is known for providing funeral and grief support services, including memorial and remembrance events.

The History of Tennessee's Oldest Funeral Home Establishment. Before the first postage stamp came into being or the first locomotive whistle was heard in Robertson County; Marion Henry had started a company that would remain a family business for 150 years- making it the oldest family funeral home in the state of Tennessee.
